Laravel Project: Job Board
Overview:
The project involves creating a job board where employers can register and post job listings.
Administrators will approve these job postings, and candidates can register to apply for jobs
either through the system or by obtaining contact details.
User Roles and Access:
Employers:
•
•
•
•
Register and create an account.
Post job listings with detailed descriptions, requirements, and benefits.
Edit and manage posted job listings.
Review and respond to applications (accept or reject).
Candidates:
•
•
•
•
•
Register and create an account.
Search for jobs using keywords, location, category, etc.
Apply for jobs by submitting their resume or directly obtaining contact details.
Manage their profile information and applications (cancel applications).
Receive notifications about job applications (bonus feature).
Admins:
•
•
Approve or reject job postings submitted by employers.
Monitor overall platform activity and user behavior.
Job Listing Management:
•
•
•
•
•
•
•
Employers can create detailed job postings including:
Job title, description, and responsibilities.
Required skills and qualifications.
Salary range and benefits offered.
Location and work type (remote, on-site, etc.).
Application deadline.
Option to upload company logos or branding elements.Application Process:
•
•
•
•
•
Candidates can apply for jobs by:
Uploading their resume directly to the platform.
Providing contact information to be forwarded to the employer (email, phone number).
Filling out application forms within the platform (bonus feature).
Employers can review applications and contact candidates directly.
Search and Filtering:
•
•
•
•
•
•
•
Candidates can search for jobs based on various criteria:
Keywords in the job title or description.
Location.
Job category or industry.
Experience level.
Salary range.
Date posted.
Bonus Features:
•
•
•
•
•
Mobile responsiveness: Ensure the platform is accessible and user-friendly on mobile
devices.
Notifications: Send alerts to users about new job postings, applications, etc.
Messaging system: Allow employers and candidates to communicate directly within the
platform (chat).
Resume database: Employers can search for specific skills or experience in a candidate
pool (optional).
Social media integration: Share job postings and attract candidates through social media
platforms.
Design: The platform should have a beautiful and intuitive design.
